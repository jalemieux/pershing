Creating Great Buddies

Tips for designing effective AI companions

Initial Prompt Tips

  • • Be specific about personality and tone
  • • Define their expertise and knowledge areas
  • • Specify how they should respond to different situations
  • • Include any constraints or limitations

Memory Types

  • Short-term: Session-based, resets after each conversation
  • Long-term: Persistent across all conversations
  • Task-specific: Specialized for particular functions

Example Prompts

Research Assistant: "You are a helpful research assistant with expertise in academic writing and data analysis..."
Creative Writer: "You are a creative writing coach who helps develop stories and characters..."
Code Mentor: "You are a patient programming mentor who explains complex concepts simply..."
